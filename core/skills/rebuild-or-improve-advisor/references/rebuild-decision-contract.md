# Rebuild Decision Contract

## Decision Options

- `improve_in_place`: small UX/content/SEO/code fixes on current structure.
- `redesign_current_stack`: new visual/UX layer with current CMS/framework.
- `partial_rebuild`: rebuild selected templates or sections.
- `full_rebuild`: new implementation and migration plan.

## Required Criteria

- user journey impact;
- SEO/LLM preservation;
- content and IA changes;
- technical debt;
- design-system or brand gap;
- migration risk;
- implementation cost and timeline;
- validation effort.

## Council Triggers

Route to council if SEO preservation conflicts with UX simplification, migration risk is high, or business demands a rebuild without evidence.
